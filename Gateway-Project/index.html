Fun Facts!


  • Population as of 2022: 2,204,000 residents

  • The city of Charlotte is named after Princess Charlotte Sophia of Mecklenburg-Strelitz, who was the Queen of Great Britain at the time the town was founded (1768).

  • The first significant Gold Rush in the United States occurred in Cabarrus County(east of Charlotte) in 1799.

  • Today, Charlotte remains the second-largest financial hub in the United States-the Queen City of Finance.

  • Charlotte, NC was one of the cities where the first Hunger Games movie was filmed.
