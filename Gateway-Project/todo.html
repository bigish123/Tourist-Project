Things to do when in Charlotte!

Let's talk Sports!


When you come vist, whether you're a fan or not, comin to see one of our proffesional teams play at home is an experience like no other. Bring your kids, your parents, your grandparents, and even your dog. When at a Panthers or Hornets game, always make sure to get a good nights rest the night before and to scream at the top of your lungs for our team. Average price for tickets are $110 for the NFL Panthers , and $86 for the Charlotte Hornets.

Carowinds


Looking for some fun under the sun? Are you the type who is not afraid of heights? Want to have some fun but for a resonable price? Well I have just the place for you. Come on down to Carowinds and enjoy our rollercoasters and water park. Carowinds has two of the 10 tallest coasters in the world and a variety of medium to low level coasters. There's a coaster for every age and every level of daring. One day admission will cost you $45, the Gold pass(good for 1 year) $125, and the Platnium pass(good for 1 year) $230. More Info.

Frankies Fun Park of Charlotte


Is being outdoors not really your style? If you are looking for a fun, day or night, activity to do with the family or loved ones, I have the place for you. Come on down to Frankie's in Charlotte for a night of FUN FUN FUN! Roller costers, go-cart racing, arcade games, and much more to enjoy. Open 7 days a week, which means fun every day of the week! More Info.

Shopping with options


Are you ready to shop till you drop? In a 1-hour radius, Charlotte welcomes you to 4 of its amazing malls. We have Northlake Mall, Concord Mills, Southpark Mall & Carolina Mall. With so many places to shop, you will never strugle to find what you are looking for. Open all year every year and very welcoming.

Lets go to the Cheesecake Factory


Life's too short for boring food, so we recommend The Cheescake Factory. Known for its large portions and expansive menu, they have more than 30 flavors of cheesecake, and offer over 200 savory breakfast, lunch, and dinner options. The Cheescake Factory is one of the top restauraunts you should visit if ever in the Queen City. Typical date nights and outtings would run you just shy of $100. More Info.
